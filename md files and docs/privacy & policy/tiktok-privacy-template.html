1. Information We Collect

We collect information from your connected social media accounts through OAuth authentication. This includes:

  • Profile Information: Username, display name, profile picture, account ID
  • Content Data: Posts, videos, captions, descriptions, tags, and metadata
  • Engagement Metrics: Likes, comments, shares, views, reactions, and other interaction data
  • Follower Statistics: Follower counts, growth trends, and audience demographics (when available)
  • Account Information: Account type, verification status, and platform-specific data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Generate analytics and performance insights
  • Display metrics, charts, and visualizations in your dashboard
  • Provide recommendations for content optimization
  • Track trends and patterns in your social media performance
  • Improve our Service and develop new features

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database with the following protections:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • OAuth tokens stored securely and encrypted
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data is only accessible to you through your authenticated account.

4. Third-Party Services

We use OAuth 2.0 to securely connect to social media platforms including:

  • YouTube (via Google OAuth)
  • TikTok
  • Facebook/Instagram (when configured)

We only access data that you explicitly authorize. We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Your social media accounts remain connected
  • You have not requested deletion

When you disconnect an account, we stop collecting new data. Historical data may be retained for analytics purposes unless you request deletion.

6. Your Rights and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have collected about your accounts
  • Disconnect: Disconnect any social media account at any time, which stops data collection
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

You can exercise these rights through the Service settings or by contacting us.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that share data with advertisers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
