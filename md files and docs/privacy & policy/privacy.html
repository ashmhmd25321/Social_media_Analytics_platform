Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Social Media Analytics Platform collects information from your connected social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, etc.) through secure OAuth authentication. This includes:

  • Profile Information: Username, display name, profile picture, account ID, and basic profile data
  • Content Data: Posts, videos, captions, descriptions, tags, and metadata from your social media accounts
  • Engagement Metrics: Likes, comments, shares, views, reactions, and other interaction data
  • Follower Statistics: Follower counts, growth trends, and audience analytics
  • Account Information: Account type, verification status, and platform-specific data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Generate analytics and performance insights for your social media accounts
  • Display metrics, charts, and visualizations in your personal dashboard
  • Provide recommendations for content optimization and best posting times
  • Track trends and patterns in your social media performance
  • Improve our Service and develop new features

We do not:

  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for any purpose other than providing analytics services

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to you through your authenticated account. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

4. OAuth and Third-Party Services

We use OAuth 2.0 to securely connect to social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, etc.). When you connect an account:

  • You authorize our app to access your account data through the platform's official OAuth process
  • We store only the access tokens necessary to fetch your analytics data
  • You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting your account in the app settings
  • We comply with each platform's API terms of service and data usage policies

We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and you have connected social media accounts. You can:

  • Disconnect any account at any time, which will stop data collection for that account
  • Delete your account, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems
  • Request data export or deletion by contacting us

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we have collected about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data or entire account
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Disconnect: Remove any connected social media account at any time
  • Opt-out: Stop data collection by disconnecting accounts

You can exercise these rights through the Service settings or by contacting us directly.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Maintaining your login state

We do not use tracking cookies, third-party analytics, or advertising cookies that share data with external services.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Notifying you through the Service if changes are material

Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your rights, or have concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us:

Email: privacy@socialmediaanalytics.com
